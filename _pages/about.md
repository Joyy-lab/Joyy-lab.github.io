Welcome to my personal website! I am Zhao Su, a PhD student at [School of Astronomy and Space Science](https://astronomy.nju.edu.cn/), [Nanjing Univeristy](https://www.nju.edu.cn/) in China. I am supervised by Prof. Zhiyuan Li. I will graduate at 2026 and hopefully become a postdoctoral researcher at [Department of Astronomy, Tsinghua University](https://astro.tsinghua.edu.cn/en).
Before pursuing the doctor degree, I achieved the B.S. degree in astronomy from Nanjing University. My research is focused on feeding and feedback of low-luminosity AGN, primarily using hydrodynamical simulations. I am also interested in intermediate-mass blacks in globular clusters and stellar feedback in the Galactic center.
